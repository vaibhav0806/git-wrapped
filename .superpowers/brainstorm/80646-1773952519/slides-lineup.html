SLIDE 8 THE SCHEDULE

Weekend Warrior Score

Percentage of your contributions that happened on weekends. Visualized as a weekday vs weekend split bar. If >30%: "You don't believe in work-life balance." If <10%: "Touch grass? Already on it."

SLIDE 9 THE NOVEL

Your Longest Commit Message

Shows the actual commit message (truncated if insane), the repo, and the character count. "You wrote a 847-character commit message. That's a blog post."

SLIDE 10 THE FINALE

Your Developer Personality

The grand finale. Based on all the stats, assigns a fun archetype: "The Nightcrawler" (late-night coder), "The Destroyer" (massive deletions), "The Machine" (long streaks), "The Socialite" (lots of PRs/reviews), etc. Shows a summary card with your top 3 traits and your archetype name in huge gradient text. Ends with "Your 2025, Unwrapped." and a prompt to export as GIF.
